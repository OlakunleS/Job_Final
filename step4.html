How To Get A Job

Résumé/Cover Letter

Getting a job requires you to submit a résumé. The reccommended résumé is black and white readable font like Arial or Seriff font. When making a résumé use eye catching titles, and include rewards achievements, special skills, employment history, volunteer experience, and education. Match your résumé to the job description. It may be tempting to use similar résumé due to the excess amount of jobs you may be applying for but it increases the chances of you getting the job. Make sure you talk in a active voice. When you are nearly finished make sure you edit and take out mistakes. State why the job would be perfect for you, and add a breif thank you note. A thank you note would thank them for the time they took reading the résumé. A lot of employers could appreciate it. When making a résumé you may want to pick a type.There are 3 most used résumés chronological, functional, or combination. Click which ever résumé you want to make for a tutorial on it.
